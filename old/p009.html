About:



Nominated cinematic demonstration video, NR Gallery is an interactive VR exhibition running within Unreal Engine 4.

Problem:



Technology is improving rapidly and with COVID-19 hitting, art galleries and exhibits alike are struggling to retain their customers, what is a way to bring back interaction with this sector of business?

Solution:



Leverage technology and create a virtual exhibit using Unreal Engine to showcase art in a physically impossible setting.

Result:



A virtual reality environment that is scalable to most devices, bringing new ways to experience art.
